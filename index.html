FREE RECIPE

VANILLA CUPCAKES

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 stick butter
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  1. Preheat your oven to 350° celcius.
  2. Carefully mix all of the above ingredients together in a mixing bowl with your choice of mixing tool.
  3. Line your cupcake tray with approximately 12 paper liners and place in the oven for approximately 25 minutes, or until golden brown.
  4. Frost your finished cupcakes with your desired frosting and enjoy the rich taste of these tasty treats!
