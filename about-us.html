Who are we?

Beau's Bonbons is a bakery and pastry shop located in the heart of Candy Mountain. We specialize in creating the most amazing, rich, and sweetest desserts and treats you will ever have in your life.

Yes, they're that good!

We were founded in 1995 by Beau himself! A legend to the pastry world. The bakery has become one of the most loved pastry shops in the city, and we welcome all for us to enjoy a sweet visit from!
